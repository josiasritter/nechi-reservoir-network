TimeSeriesData_2000-2015:
Compilation of time series data in daily time step (years 2000-2015) freely available from XM Compañia de Expertos en Mercados (http://informacioninteligente10.xm.com.co/hidrologia/Paginas/HistoricoHidrologia.aspx)

Daily values of:
- Natural reservoir inflows from drainage areas (does not include inflows from upstream dam releases)
- Reservoir storages (and associated water levels)
- Reservoir spills
- Power generation
